pub struct Scalar<S: ScalarNumber>(pub S);
Expand description

Scalar wraper types.

This type support some operators based on overload.

  • a + b
  • a += b
  • a - b
  • a -= b
  • a * b
  • a *= b
  • a == b
  • a != b
